About

February 3, 2023 · 2 min · huscous

﷽

In early 2023, with the grace and mercy of Allah, I decided to enrol onto an Alimiyah cohort that was half way through the second year.

What you may or may not see:

  • notes 1
  • the physical (or digital) manifestations of people’s duas for me
  • musings
  • poetry (in all multiple languages)

Most students do not keep a diary - certainly not a public one, but I figure this would be a nice time capsule for me, my teachers, and my peers to look back at and be grateful for. To see myself hopefully being lifted away from the darkness and into the light.

I was inspired to do this by a Twitter account called DarsDiary, but I think Twitter is too noisy for this kind of content. Ergo, ayn.

All feedback is much appreciated.

Why ع ?#

  • The name of first dictionary in Arabic (written by the al-Khalil)
  • To know - علم - يعلم - a word that begins with ع

How ع ?#

I built ayn using hugo, deployed on netlify. Everything you see will be written in pandoc, to allow for easy conversion into LaTeX/PDFs.
