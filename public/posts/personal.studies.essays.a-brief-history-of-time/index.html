A Brief History of Time

February 6, 2023 · 2 min · 303 words · huscous

﷽

All praise is for Allah, The One who sends down rain from the heavens, onto dry and barren land, bringing forth from it a tree - whose roots are fimly planted in the ground and branches reach far into the sky 1. The One who created the darkness of the night and the brilliance of the moon 2 to guide the travellers.

The night truly was dark, when the first verses of al-Alaq were revealed, but the brilliance of the moon ﷺ was to be seen for all mankind to witness and to be guided by. The revelation of God’s (uncreated) words onto the messenger ﷺ is His mercy for mankind; for the first time in 600 odd years (and the last time in history) if one had the blessing of being in the company of the beloved ﷺ, they could find the answer to any question and the solution to any problem, from God Himself. The answer would be Truth (with a capital T) - there is no epistemological uncertainty associated with it (لا شكّ ولا ريب فيه). With the veiling of the messenger ﷺ from this world, that door would close.

The next 1400 years, humanity will leave no stone unturned and exhaust all its effort to attempt to find truth of the same ilk, quality and epistemic certainty, but to no avail. This is that story.
