Speech#

الكَلاَمُ (٢) هُوَ اللَّفْظُ المُرَكَّبُ المُفِيدُ بِالوَضْعِ

Speech according to the grammarians consists of these 4 things. - إصطلحي.

  • لفظ excludes things like إشارات 1 (gestures), writings, or signs (like traffic lights)
  • مركب excludes singluar words
  • مفيد excludes phrases that give no benefit (can the speaker stop after the speech has ended such that the one who is addressed is not waiting for more information?) 2

  1. Speech linguistically can be gestures: فأشارت إليه قالوا كيف نكلم من كان في المهد صبيّا, or diagrams or signs. Not necessarily uttered ↩︎

  2. “If zayd”. This sentence has more than one word, but is not مفيد ↩︎
