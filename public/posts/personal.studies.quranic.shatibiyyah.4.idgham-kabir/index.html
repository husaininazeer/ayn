Idghaam Kabir - إدغام كبير

January 29, 2022 · 3 min · huscous

This issue arises in Fatiha, in الرحمن الرحيم - ملك يوم الدين , but Shatibi doesn’t expand on it in that chapter - there’s too much to mention (unlike Silah of meem jam` - which he does expand on)

116 - وَدُونَكَ الاُِدْغَامَ الْكَبِيرَ وَقُطْبُهُ … أَبُو عَمْرٍو الْبَصْرِيُّ فِيهِ تَحَفَّلَا

And take Idgham Kabir’s Qutb (leader), Abu `Amr al-Basri in (who’s rewayah) it has been compiled

  • وَقُطْبُهُ literally means pole or axis - so the leader that everything revolves around

  • فِيهِ تَحَفَّلَا in Abu `Amr’s rewayah it has been compiled or this chapter concerns him - أي في هذا الباب احتفى و اهتمّ

  • أَبُو عَمْرٍو الْبَصْرِيُّ In reality it’s just Susi rather than Doori (in Shatibiyyah who does Idgham Kabir)

Definition of Idgham#

  • لغةً : when one thing enters another
  • إصطلاحاً:

The pronouncing of two letters as one letter with a shaddah instead of there being two elevations (or movements) of the tongue, there will be one -Imam Sakhāwi

It is not the entering or mixing (إدخال الحرف إلى الحرف آخر) of one letter into another 1

Classifying different types of Idgham#

  1. Kabir: both are with Harakat
  2. Saghir: first is ساكن and second is متحرك
  • called صغير as there is less to do to achieve the إدغام
    • و بالعكس للإدغام الكبير

Reasons for (and further classification of the two types of) إدغام:

  • 2 letters of the same letter - متماثلين

  • 2 letters of similar exit points but varying in traits - متقاربين

  • 2 letters of similar traits but different exit points - متجانسين

  • saghir (with its 3 sub types) is present with all qurra'

  • As for متباعدين - two letters which are distant in مخرج , there is no إدغام for them e.g. نْ + حروف الحلق

Idgham Kabir in one word#

117 - فَفِي كِلْمَةٍ عَنْهُ مَنَاسِككُّمْ وَمَا … سَلَككُّمْ وَبَاقِي الْبَابِ لَيْسَ مُعَوَّلَا

And in one word مناسككم and ما سلككم the rest of the chapter does not rely on it

  • مَنَاسِككُّمْ Some read the poem with silah 2, (to not break the meter or the flow?) but no-one actually does silah with Idgham kabir, so it might be better to keep meem jam` as sakin

  • these two are the only 2 words with إدغام كبير المثلين في كلمة واحدة

    • even though there are others which would qualify (like فإنك بأعيننا، فتكوى بها جباههم etc..)

Idgham Kabir between two words#

118 - وَمَا كَانَ مِنْ مِثْلَيْنِ فِي كِلْمَتَيْهِمَا … فَلاَ بُدَّ مِنْ إدْغَامِ مَا كانَ أَوَّلَا

As for مثلين in two two words there is no doubt there is إدغام like the first

119 - كَيَعْلَمُ مَا فِيهِ هُدًى وَطُبِعْ عَلَى … قُلُوبِهِمُ وَالْعَفْوَ وَأْمُرْ تَمَثَّلَا

Like in يَعْلَمُ مَا, فِيهِ هُدًى, طُبِعْ عَلَى قُلُوبِهِمُ
